Definition

The Earth is the planet we're on, the third planet of the Solar System.

Movement
...

The earth has 4 types of movement: Rotation, Revolution, Precession, and Nutation.

Rotation

The Earth rotates around its Axis from West to East.

Revolution

The Earth moves in an Orbit around the Sun.

Precession

The Earth's Axis rotates. (Takes very long, about 25,771.5 years).

Nutation

The Earth's Axis has a back and forth movement. A small Oscillation that takes 18.6 years.

Earth's Coordinate System
...

The Earth's Coordinate System is divided into two values, Latitude and Longitude.

Longitude

Longitude is the Horizontal Coordinate, which goes around the Earth's Axis, as the sun takes 24 hours to go around the Celestial Sphere, and there are 360º of Longitude around the Earth, we can say that each degree of Longitude is worth hours time-wise. Longitude starts at 0º on the Prime Meridian, in Greenwich. The direction is indicated by the letters E or W, East or West. Remember that the Earth rotates from West to East.

Latitude

Latitude is the Vertical Coordinate, it starts on 0º on the Equator Line and then can go North or South, indicated by the letters N or S.

Earth's Tropics
...

The Tropics are divisions by Latitude that divide the Earth into 5 zones.

Remember that the Cancer comes Before the Capricorn. (Alphabetical order)
