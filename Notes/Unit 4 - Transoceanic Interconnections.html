4.3 Columbian Exchange
...

The Columbian Exchange

It changed the world ABSURDLY: a disaster for the natives and wealth for the colonizers.

What was it?

Fundamentally it was the transfer of people, animals, and diseases from the East to the West, and vice versa.

Before Columbus invaded the Americas, the continents were alien to each other and totally isolated from one another. And thus, when the white people came from the East to the natives, a bunch of diseases got them killed as they did not have.

Diseases

The more Europeans that came to the west, the more diseases they brought with them.

Effects on Belief System
...

Syncretism - Combining different religious beliefs and practices

Santeria “The way of the saints” African + Roman Catholicism, commonly found in Cuba

Vodun (Voodoo)- “spirit” or “deity” This is the belief system originating with the Dahomey, Kongo, and Yoruba people who were enslaved in Saint Domingue (Hati)

Candomble- “dance to honor the gods” Yoruba, Fon, and Bantu beliefs and developed in Brazil.

**Please note that these rarely manifest as religions but as movements within a major religion like Catholicism. Most people would identify as Catholic, yet have practices that are “official” Catholic practices.

Cult of the Madonna
